Vasco Corporation is a secretive megacorp whose true agenda goes far beyond finance. Using its immense resources in science, technology and shadow diplomacy, it leads projects aimed at discovering the existence of parallel universes and exploiting interdimensional rifts. While presenting itself as a forward-thinking research conglomerate, its methods are often ruthless.

  • Focus: Multiverse research, interdimensional travel, advanced technologies
  • Front Companies: Vasco Labs, Vasco Finance, Vasco Defense
  • Key Projects: Project Gate, Mirror Initiative, Quantum Corridor
  • Alignment: Officially neutral — secretly manipulative
