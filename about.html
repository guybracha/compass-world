About Me

I'm Guy Bracha — a storyteller, designer, and full-stack tinkerer behind Compass World. I build worlds, draw heroes, and ship features. My focus: clean interfaces, readable code, and bold, cinematic visuals that feel alive.

When I'm not sketching or writing arcs for the Compass Alliance, you'll find me refining React frontends, sculpting Firebase data models, or experimenting with Blender scenes and stylized renders. I love making complex ideas feel simple, playful, and human.
