Prime-Children

A generation of power. A world reshaped.

What are the Prime-Children?

Prime-Children are a generation of humans who began to emerge in the early 2020s with distinct physiological traits and superhuman abilities. Their appearance coincides with a global disruption period and is reshaping culture, policy, and power balances across the world.

Core Traits

  • Unusual phenotype: eye glow, atypical skin hues/patterns, hair luminescence, vestigial markings.
  • Power domains: elemental control (fire/water/air/stone), bio-electrical fields, tele/psy abilities, matter shaping, kinetic amplification, and rare time/space anomalies.
  • Stability curve: abilities spike under stress, then plateau as control and training improve.
  • Energetic signature: detectable by advanced sensors (e.g., Interguard arrays) as coherent bio-frequency bands.

First Emergence & Timeline

  • Early indicators: isolated cases during global lockdowns (2020).
  • Public awareness: media & scientific confirmation of clusters (2020–2021).
  • Conflict flashpoint: May 2022 – “Developers” alien incursions targeting Prime-Children.
  • Policy response: establishment of Interguard (post-2022) to monitor and regulate cases.

Triggers & Manifestation

  • Onset: typically adolescence; a sudden “first surge” under emotional or environmental stress.
  • Training vectors: meditation, biofeedback, mentor systems, team drills; uncontrolled expression decreases with practice.
  • Limits: power fatigue, feedback burns, sensory overload; long recovery after maximal output.

Societal Impact

  • Public sentiment: a mix of awe and fear; debates on rights, identity, and duty.
  • Institutions: schools & clinics adapt; specialized response units; surveillance controversies.
  • Geopolitics: arms race over detection/containment tech; recruitment by states & off-world actors.
